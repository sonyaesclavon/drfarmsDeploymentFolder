HEALTH REASONS TO EAT BLUEBERRIES:

A daily bowl of blueberries could protect against obesity, heart disease and diabetes.

Eating delicious blueberries can add years to your life.

Blueberries are low in calories & high in fiber, vitamin C and vitamin K.

Eating a regular consumption of berries can improve or prevent metabolic syndrome. (This is the medical term for a combination of diabetes, high blood pressure and obesity.)

Blueberries contain substances that can prevent certain bacteria from binding to the wall of your bladder helping prevent UTIs.

Eating a bowl of wild blueberries a day could protect against a range of health problems including obesity, heart disease and diabetes.

Several studies suggest that blueberries and blueberry juice reduce DNA damage, which is a leading driver of aging and cancer.

The antioxidants in blueberries seem to benefit your brain by aiding brain function and delaying mental decline.

They boost your heart health, brain function and numerous other aspects of your body.
